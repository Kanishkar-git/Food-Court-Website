About Us

Welcome to JKL Food Court! At JKL Food Court, we believe that food is more than just a meal—it's an experience.

Nestled in the heart of the city, our food court is a celebration of diverse culinary traditions, crafted with passion and served with a smile.

Our journey began with a shared vision between three friends—Kanishkar, Kamalesh, and John Antony—who are united by their love for great food and a commitment to creating a vibrant dining space. Each of us brings a unique flavor to the table, from innovative ideas and culinary expertise to exceptional service and hospitality.

Kanishkar is the creative force behind our food court, always looking for ways to push the boundaries of flavor and presentation. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for perfection, Kanishkar ensures that every dish we serve is nothing short of extraordinary.

Kamalesh brings a wealth of experience in food management and operations. His dedication to maintaining high standards and his strategic approach to running the food court help us deliver a seamless dining experience every day. Kamalesh’s commitment to excellence ensures that everything runs smoothly behind the scenes.

John Antony is our culinary visionary, with a deep understanding of global cuisines and a flair for creating mouthwatering dishes. John’s expertise in menu development and his innovative approach to cooking elevate our food court’s offerings, making each visit a delightful adventure for the taste buds.

Together, we’ve created a space where you can explore a variety of flavors, from local favorites to international delights. Whether you’re here for a quick bite or a leisurely meal, our goal is to provide a welcoming atmosphere where you can savor delicious food and make lasting memories.

Thank you for visiting JKL Food Court. We look forward to serving you and making every meal an unforgettable experience!

Warmest regards,
Kanishkar, Kamalesh, and John Antony
